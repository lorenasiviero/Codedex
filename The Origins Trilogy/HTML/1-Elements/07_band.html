The Neighbouthood 🏠

About

Melancholy California sunsets turned into sound — that is the arrow-straight vibe The Neighbourhood has been shooting since 2011.
Fusing alternative rock with shadowy R&B textures, their grayscale aesthetic and slow-burn hooks (“Sweater Weather,” anyone?) made
them Tumblr-legendary and Billboard-relevant all at once. The band is currently on hiatus, while front-man Jesse Rutherford pursues
solo adventures, but their catalog still feels like late-night neon.

Members

  • Jesse Rutherford - vocals
  • Jeremy Freedman - lead/rhythm guitar
  • Zach Abels - lead/rhythm guitar
  • Michael Margott - bass
